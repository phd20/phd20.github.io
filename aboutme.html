I'm a huge fan of Dungeons & Dragons. I've been playing and DMing since 2010. As a Dungeon Master, I've run hundreds of sessions and my longest campaign was 7 years. I entered the hobby during D&D 4th Edition and currently play and DM 5th Edition. My experience with other RPGs includes Star Wars: Force and Destiny, AD&D, Fate Core, Pathfinder, and A Song of Ice and Fire RPG.

Over hundreds of sessions (and thousands of DM hours), my friends and I have created a wonderful setting for our stories. If you're interested in scanning the archives of our adventures and world, check out the Campaigns menu above.

I'm a self-taught software engineer turned Engineering Manager. I love my day job and I'm blessed to work with such amazing and talented people each and every day.

I love to read. If you're interested in what I'm reading, check out my Goodreads. I sometimes update it.

I'm landlocked in Nebraska in the United States. I enjoy it here but hope to see far more of the world than I already have.

I also have an old YouTube channel, covering all things D&D.
