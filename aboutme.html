Greetings! My goal is to provide resources to help you build a better TTRPG experience. Here you'll find maps, articles, and products to help level up your game. Browse the site and please hit me up on Twitter if there's something you'd like to see.

Be sure to subscribe to the newsletter so you don't miss out on new resources.

Who Am I?

Hi! My name is Kirk and I'm a huge fan of Dungeons & Dragons. I've been playing and DMing since 2010. As a Dungeon Master, I've run hundreds of sessions and my longest campaign was 7 years. I entered the hobby during D&D 4th Edition and currently play and DM 5th Edition. My experience with other RPGs includes Star Wars: Force and Destiny, AD&D, Fate Core, Pathfinder, and A Song of Ice and Fire RPG.

I'm a self-taught software engineer turned Engineering Manager. I love my day job and I'm blessed to work with such amazing and talented people each and every day.

I love to read. If you're interested in what I'm reading, check out my Goodreads. I sometimes update it.

I'm landlocked in Nebraska in the United States. I enjoy it here but hope to see far more of the world than I already have.

I also have an old YouTube channel, covering all things D&D.
